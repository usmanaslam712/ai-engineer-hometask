# Approved help-center answers (the ONLY policy the bot may state)

> These are the approved answers for "PayWallet" support. The bot may explain only what's here.
> If a question isn't covered, the bot must NOT invent policy, fees, timelines, or steps.

## Freezing / unfreezing a card
You can freeze or unfreeze your card yourself in the app: open **Current Balance → your card → Freeze card**.
A frozen card blocks all new transactions until you unfreeze it.

## Card declined / online payment failing
A declined online payment is most often a temporary issue on the merchant's side, not a problem with the card.
Advise the customer to try again after a few hours (ideally 24h). If it keeps failing, collect the merchant
name, amount, and date/time so it can be looked into.

## Refund timeline
A refund shows as "refunded" once the merchant has processed it, but it can take a few working days to appear in
the balance, and the exact timing depends on the merchant. There is no fixed PayWallet timeline.

## OTP not received
OTPs are sent by SMS to the registered mobile number only — never via WhatsApp or email. If abroad, the customer
must enable international roaming on that SIM. If the SIM is lost, identity must be verified before anything else.

## Tap to pay
Tap to pay works only with a **physical** card at a payment terminal. It is **not** available on virtual cards.

## Account deletion
Acknowledge warmly. Important caveat: once an account is deleted, the customer cannot sign up again with the same
national ID. Any remaining balance must be transferred out first. Deletion completes in 7–10 working days.
